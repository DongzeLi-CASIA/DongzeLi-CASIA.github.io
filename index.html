I am a Ph.D. Candidate of CRIPAC (led by Prof. Tieniu Tan), National Laboratory of Pattern Recognition (NLPR), Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CASIA) under the supervision of Prof. Tieniu Tan and Dr. Wei Wang.
